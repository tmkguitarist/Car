Even today the RX-7 is still ranked as one of the best handling vehicles ever produced. For a rear-wheel drive it was able to have the control closely to that of an all-wheel drive. Built on the FD3S chassis, the RX-7 was produced from 1992 to 2002 and was equipped with a 1.3 liter 13B-REW twin-turbocharged engine. The turbochargers play an important part in how this car operates. Usually turbos will kick in at a higher RPM whereas for the RX-7 has one for low RPM and the other for the top-end. This is a feature not many cars are equipped with.
