One of our more pricey vehicles, the Toyota Supra along with the Skyline R34 is one of the most iconic Japanese cars that is highly sought after. The 3 liter inline 6 2JZ-GTE is capable of a very high power output, being able to potentially reach over 1,000 horsepower on stock internals. The model we are offering here is completely stock and kept up to date on all servicing. The fourth generation Supra would be produced from 1993 to 2002. After being tested on the A70 chassis before it, Toyota developed the A80 chassis which is the one pictured above. Similar to other vehicles like the R34 and the EVO, the Supra would also see itself in different forms of media like the racing video games Gran Turismo and Forza, and of course the main vehicle in the first Fast and the Furious movie.
