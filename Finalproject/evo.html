From 2003 to 2005, the Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution (EVO) VIII was produced following the same CT9A chassis that the EVO VII had before it. As the VIII suggests, this EVO was the eigth generation and the first that would also be manufactured in the United States. For the engine, a 2.0 liter 4G63 inline-four used in previous models made its return. A noticeable appearance from this car was in the movie Fast and the Furious Tokyo Drift where it boasted a major supporting role for the protagonist learning how to drift.
