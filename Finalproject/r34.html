Building off of the previous Skyline, the R34 GT-R was produced from 1999 to 2002 as a two-door coupé that has gained a massive amount of love in the car community over the years. With the love has came a massive inflation in price due to how sought after they have become in recent years. Paul Walker's R34 in 2 Fast 2 Furious and features in games like Gran Turismo and the Forza franchise definitely does not help much. Just like the Skyline before it, the R34 kept many things the same such as the engine and the ATTESA E-TS Pro all-wheel drive system. For the transmission, a 6-speed was put in over a 5-speed.
