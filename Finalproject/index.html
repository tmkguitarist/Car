Welcome to Import on Demand



Welcome to Import on demand, one of the top places to purchase a vehicle straight from Japan. Import on demand was established in 1996 in Matsuyama, Ehime Prefecture in Japan. Since then we have sold over one thousand vehicles, mainly cars. To date, we have worked with over 50 countries and are consistently expanding to many more. In 2015, a country that has been isolated for far too long from receiving these vehicles due to strict import clauses finally opened up for business. The United States became one of our highest ratest countries to work with.

Buying a vehicle here can ensure the following:

  • Free inspection before shipping
  • Free holding in Japan as needed
  • Paperwork filled out for port of entry
  • Mobile alerts for all movement of the car
